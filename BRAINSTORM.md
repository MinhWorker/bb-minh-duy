# Brainstorming: HTX Bồn Bồn Minh Duy

## Primary Goal
To transform the **HTX Bồn Bồn Minh Duy** website from a static showcase into a **dynamic cooperative platform** that emphasizes human connection, social impact, and regional heritage (Cà Mau OCOP), while optimizing for local B2C and B2B commerce.

## Milestones

### 1. The Human Element (Trust & Transparency)
*   **Farmer Profiles:** Integrate member-farmer stories into the landing page and product details.
*   **Impact Metrics:** Display live or updated counters for community and environmental impact.

### 2. Commercial Optimization (Engagement & Conversion)
*   **Direct Commerce:** Implement "Order via Zalo" direct-to-chat links for seamless local ordering.
*   **Interactive Content:** Add printable/shareable recipe cards and video "Process Tours".

### 3. Origin & Stewardship (Regional Pride)
*   **Traceability:** Digital "Seed to Jar" timeline and Certificate of Origin showcase.
*   **Ecosystem Education:** Content highlighting the unique Cà Mau wetland environment.

## Three Most Important Questions
1.  **Humanizing the HTX:** Do you have photos and short stories/profiles for the individual member-farmers ready, or should we design the system to handle placeholders/generic collective stories initially?
2.  **Impact Metrics:** Which "Social Impact" metric would resonate most with your audience: the number of families supported, the hectares of wetlands preserved, or the OCOP quality standard achievements?
3.  **Commerce Flow:** Should the "Order via Zalo" button trigger a generic chat, or would you prefer it to pre-populate a specific message with the product name and price to streamline the HTX's response?
